ECE 383 - Final Project

The final project in this class will be the design and implementation of an engineering prototype to address some need. The idea is to utilize what you have learned this semester with the Atlys Board to interface with a new I/O device in a new and innovative way. The final projects in this class are to be completed individually. The following are examples of projects completed in past years.
