Introduction to the Digilent Nexys Video

A great way to get an introduction to your development board is to watch this video. Of special note is that the Nexys Video has a massive amount of RAM - 512 MB! Considering computers often have only a few GB, that is a significant amount of memory. However, the interface to this DRAM needs to be set up by you, the programmer of the FPGA.
